About The Company

SkyLoran Dynamics is a company engaged in telecommunications engineering, maritime navigation systems, and the development of low-frequency radio infrastructure (Low Frequency Navigation Systems). The company possesses official legal authorization and adequate project experience, enabling it to have the technical and administrative capacity to carry out the construction of an eLORAN Master Station (MS).

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading company in the development of reliable, precise, and sustainable low-frequency navigation and telecommunications infrastructure systems, supporting maritime safety and national navigation resilience.

Mission:

  • To deliver innovative and integrated telecommunications engineering solutions aligned with international standards.
  • To strengthen national navigation resilience through robust GNSS backup systems.
  • To continuously enhance expertise and technological capabilities through research and development.
